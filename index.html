Email Address Validator

Dark Mode

Enter email addresses to validate, one per line. You can use either "email@example.com" or "Name <email@example.com>" format.

The validator checks email format, DNS records, and can filter role-based, disposable, or unlikely valid emails.

Allow role-based email addresses
Role-based email address means emails such as info@example.com and sales@example.com, that is, email inbox that is relating to a role within an organization, not a person.
Allow disposable email addresses
Disposable email addresses, also known as throw-away emails and one-time-use emails, are email addresses provided by special websites that are intended to be used only for a short time.
Allow unlikely valid email addresses
Unlikely valid email addresses are email addresses such as "nothanks@example.com" and "nospam@example.com" which could be valid, but probably are not.
